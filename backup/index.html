Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can AtiendeBot be set up?

AtiendeBot can be set up and running within 24-48 hours. Our team handles the entire integration process, including WhatsApp Business API setup and bot configuration.

Can the bot handle multiple languages?

Yes! AtiendeBot supports multiple languages including English, Spanish, Portuguese, and more. You can configure different languages for different customer segments.

What happens when the bot can't answer a question?

When AtiendeBot encounters a question it can't handle, it automatically escalates the conversation to a human agent. You'll receive a notification to take over the conversation.

Is there a contract or can I cancel anytime?

No long-term contracts required! You can cancel your subscription at any time. We also offer a 30-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied.
